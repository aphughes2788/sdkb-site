Membership and Fees

San Diego Kendo Bu (SDKB) is a proud member of the Southern California Kendo Federation (SCKF) and All United States Kendo Federation (AUSKF).

To join San Diego Kendo Bu, click here to fill out the new membership packet. The packet includes the following:

  • San Diego Kendo Bu New Member Application
  • San Diego Kendo Bu Liability Waiver
  • SCKF Member Application
  • AUSKF Member Application

After you have joined, we recommend reading this primer on kendo etiquette.

Please refer to this beginners skill goals checklist for an idea on what you can expect to learn in your first stages of practice.

Membership Fees:

Federation Dues: Annual fees for SCKF and AUSKF are collected by the dojo. These fees cover the cost of liability insurance and grant access to rank testing.

Dojo Fees: Dojo fees are collected on a quarterly basis. Fees go towards liability insurance and dojo property use. The dojo fees for SDKB are amongst the lowest in any kendo dojo nationally, with the greatest number of opportunities for practice weekly offered by any dojo in California.

(Membership period runs from 1st of April on the current year until the end of March the following year. Fees are not pro-rated).

Additional Costs:

  • Shinai (training weapon): $20
  • Gi/Hakama: See the "About Gi and Hakama" section on our Kendo equipment page for details and links
  • Bogu: See the "About Bogu" section section on our Kendo equipment page for details and links
